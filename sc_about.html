About Us!

Spanish Club is open to any student who is currently enrolled in Spanish or who has completed four years of study. The club has meetings at 7:40 a.m. in room 114 (we always have donuts ☺) and we try to do many events throughout the year. Spanish club is a really good time! If you have any other questions or are interested in joining, ask your spanish for a membership form (If you decide to sign up, please return $2.00 with your membership form). Any additional information can be obtained by contacting Mr. Gann or Mrs. Lipnick. Please consider signing up and can't wait to see you!
