Club Events




Tostada Fiesta

When:

Generally sometime in September

Where:

In a club member's backyard

Information:

This Tostada Fiesta has been an ongoing tradition at West for 50 years now! We get our tostadas from Los Primos and all gather in a club member's big backyard and enjoy our tostadas with some pop. We also play some volleyball, some "two-hand touch" football, and have some fun competition between classes (Fresh, Soph, Jrs, Srs).




Chicago Trip

When:

Generally sometime in May

Where:

Chicago

Information:

We try to do the Chicago trip every year. While we are in Chicago, we go to the Art Institute and the Mexican Fine Arts Museum. Everyone is free to do their own thing, like shopping, eating etc. We go to the bean as a class. It is just a really fun time!




Pizza Parties

When:

Anytime

Where:

Room 114

Information:

We order a pizza from where ever the club members decide and we gather in room 114 with drinks and just visit and have fun!




Other Events and Activities

Scavenger Hunts, Cosmo Disco Bowling, Go to a mexican restaurant, and any other activities the club members come up with.
